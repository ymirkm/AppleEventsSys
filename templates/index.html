Linens

We specialize in affordable, elegant and custom tablecloths, napkins, spandex, chair covers and overlays for any occasion. Our unique selection of linens comes from searching the latest trends in fabrics and textures around the world.

We provide and manage your linen inventory and ensure availability upon needs. We launder, treat and remove tough stains; but ensure linens maintain a new look at all times. Rather than have to worry about purchasing linens on a periodic form, we will make our wide assortment of linens available to you for one low rental/maintenance cost. And to give you a competitive advantage, we can even custom-make linens for your venue to ensure that color schemes, textures and special needs are met.
