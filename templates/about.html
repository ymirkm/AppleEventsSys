ABOUT US

Organization

Apple Event Productions (AEP) is an event production company that has been serving the South Florida market for over 21 years. Our key focus is on providing our clients with an opportunity to beautify and create memorable experiences through our event designs and customized services.

Our Services include: Event Planning, Floral Décor, Fine Linens & Spandex (Rentals & Sales) and various Party Rental Supplies (Chiavari Chairs, Lounge Furniture, Draping, Charger Plates and more.). We also provide a customized “Boutique Linen Service” to select Hotel & Restaurant clients that includes Supplying & Maintenance of Table Linens, Runners & Napkins.

“We are dedicated to meeting the individual needs of all our clients”

From Weddings to Corporate Events, our Fine Linen selection is sure to make a hit and bring out life to your special event. We make it a point to stay up-to-date with fashion trends & styles, in order to ensure that your event is elegant and chic. And if we don’t have it within the thousands of linens in our inventory, we will manufacture it within our in-house Designs.

Our attention to detail, experience, and passion for design will help you transform your dream into reality.
